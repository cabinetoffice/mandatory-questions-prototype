Test Grant for Prototyping

The Test Grant for Prototyping (TGP) offers no funding. It exists for the purpose of testing the Find a Grant service, and does not exist on the real Find a Grant website. The scheme was re-launched in June 2023 to enable more people to trial the prototype and help learn more about how people approach and feel about the experience of applying for a grant.

  • Opening date: 30 April 2023, 11:00am
  • Closing date: 1 July 2023, 11:59pm
