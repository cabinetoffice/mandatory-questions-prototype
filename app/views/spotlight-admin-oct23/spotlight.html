About Spotlight

The Government Grants Management Function and Counter Fraud Team have developed Spotlight. This is a due diligence tool that can automate basic due diligence checks.

Spotlight enables you to:

  • conduct basic checks quicker
  • identify areas of potential risk in received applications
  • match large volumes of applicants against pre-defined risk indicators

It can also conduct a Post Award Assurance check.
This confirms whether the recipient has hit a number of Key Risk Indicators (KRIs). These KRIs reflect the potential fraud risk of an organisation in relation to grant funding and should be used by the user to assess where further investigation and intervention may be required.
